Our Vision

A community where every person values, understands and respects human rights and equal opportunity enshrined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights and other international human rights standards

Our Mission

To achieve our vision, we secure and empower the rights of people, eliminate discrimination, investigate human rights abuses and pressure those with power to respect rights and secure justice.

Core Values

Our work is guided by international human rights and humanitarian law and respect for the dignity of each human being. We will participate in, contribute to and gain from the Network of Youth Defender and the work of other Human Rights Organization. We will treat one another as equals, with mutual trust, respect, and solidarity. We will be open, transparent and reliable in our decisions and relationships. We will communicate with and about each other in a respectful, non-violent and nonaggressive manner.

Reporting Incident

Every person is entitled to certain fundamental rights, simply by the fact of being human. Raise a concern and help the authorities to identify patterns and trends in the incidents reported in order to improve the way we respond to reports.

About Youth Defender

Youth Defender is a joint program of Network For Human Rights & Justice (NHRJ) and Youth For Pakistan (YFP). Its primary objectives are to promote and protect Human Rights and to help the poor and down trodden in securing their rights through advocacy, research, legal aid and financial assistance. Youth Defender with this leverage bring all national organization, institutions and human rights defenders to press for change in policy and practice that promote and implement declarations, covenants, protocols and communiqués related to Human Rights and justice around the country.
